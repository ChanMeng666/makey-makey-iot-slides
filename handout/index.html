Build a Banana Piano

Make a fun toy with a free AI tool, then play it with a Makey Makey board.

What you need

  • A laptop with a web browser.
  • A free AI tool: open one in your browser, or install Cursor (free for students).
  • Later: a Makey Makey board, alligator clips, and a banana, foil, or playdough.

Three steps

  1. Open the AI tool and paste the prompt below.
  2. Run it and play your toy with the keyboard.
  3. Clip on a banana and make it yours.
The prompt to paste Make a fun web-page piano. When I press Space, the arrow keys, or W A S D F G, play a different musical note and flash a big colourful button for that key on the screen. Make it work with a normal keyboard.

Makey Makey key map

W A S D F GBack of the board

Makey Makey pretends to be a keyboard, so touching a banana is the same as pressing a key. Your code does not change at all.

Make it yours

  • Ask the AI to use your own sounds (a drum kit).
  • Add a score, or turn it into a small game.
  • Change the colours and the key labels.
Safety: only use food, foil, and playdough. Hold the metal Earth bar so the circuit works. Never connect anything to mains electricity.
